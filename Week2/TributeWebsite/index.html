Biography

Cecily von Ziegesar is an American author best known for the young adult series "Gossip Girl." The series, which explores the lives of privileged teenagers in New York City, became a cultural phenomenon and was adapted into a successful TV show.

  • You Know You Love Me (2002)
  • All I Want Is Everything (2003)
  • Because I'm Worth It (2003)
  • I Like It Like That (2004)
  • The Carlyles (2008)
